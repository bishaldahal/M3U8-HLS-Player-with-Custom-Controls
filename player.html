Keyboard Shortcuts

  • k or j Toggle play/pause
  • Left Arrow Rewind 10 seconds
  • Right Arrow Fast forward 10 seconds
  • p (SHIFT + p) Previous video
  • n (SHIFT + n) Next video
  • < (SHIFT + ,) Previous frame (while paused)
  • > (SHIFT + .) Next frame (while paused)
  • 0 .. 9 Decrease playback rate
  • . .. 9 Increase playback rate
  • 7 Seek to specific point in the video (7 advances to 70% of duration)
  • [ Seek to previous chapter
  • ] Seek to next chapter
  • Control + Left Arrow Previous frame (while playing)
  • Control + Right Arrow Next frame (while playing)
  • f Toggle full screen
  • t Toggle theatre mode
  • i Toggle miniplayer
  • Esc Close miniplayer or current dialogue
  • m Toggle mute
