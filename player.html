Keyboard Shortcuts

  • k Toggle play/pause
  • j Rewind 10 seconds
  • l Fast forward 10 seconds
  • P (SHIFT + p) Previous video
  • N (SHIFT + n) Next video
  • < (SHIFT + ,) Previous frame (while paused)
  • >(SHIFT + .) Next frame (while paused)
  • 0..9 Decrease playback rate
  • CONTROL + → Increase playback rate
  • CONTROL + ← Seek to specific point in the video (7 advances to 70% of duration)
  • Seek to previous chapter -
  • Seek to next chapter -
  • f Toggle full screen
  • t Toggle theatre mode
  • i Toggle miniplayer
  • ESCAPE Close miniplayer or current dialogue
  • m Toggle mute
